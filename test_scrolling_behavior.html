Combobox Scrolling Test

Manual Test Instructions:

  1. Click "Open Test Page" to open the application
  2. Click on the country selector combobox to open it
  3. Use the down arrow key to navigate to the bottom of the visible area
  4. Continue pressing down arrow when you reach the bottom
  5. Expected behavior: The combobox should scroll down one line at a time, keeping the selected item at the bottom of the viewport
  6. Previous problem: The combobox was scrolling half a page and putting the selected item in the middle

What to Look For:

  • Smooth, single-line scrolling (not jumping half a page)
  • Selected item stays at the bottom edge of the viewport when scrolling down
  • Selected item stays at the top edge of the viewport when scrolling up
  • No jarring jumps or large scrolling movements
